# App Bar

![](/img/appbar.png)

## **Anatomy**

**Elements:**

1. **Status bar** – Shows time, signal, Wi‑Fi, and battery.

2. **Navigation icon** – Back or close button.

3. **Title** – Screen title or input field for search.

4. **Trailing icon 1** – Layout toggle (grid/list).

5. **Trailing icon 2** – Additional actions (optional).

6. **Filter button** – Opens filter menu.

7. **Filter chips** – Sorting options (e.g., Popular).

8. **Input field** – Replaces title in search mode.

9. **Cancel button** – Appears during search to exit mode.

***

## **Properties**

### **Property 1 – Title Type**

- **Large text** – Wishlist (bold heading style).

- **Search** – Input replaces title.

- **Default** – Standard headline text.

### **Property 2 – Trailing Icon 1**

- Grid or list view toggle (optional).

### **Property 3 – Trailing Icon 2**

- Secondary action (optional).

### **Property 4 – Show Filter**

- Displays horizontal filter chips.

### **Property 5 – Show Filter Button**

- Displays filter icon before chips.

### **Property 6 – Show Title**

- Toggles visibility of headline (hidden in search mode).

***

## **Variants**

- **Default** – Title with trailing icons.

- **Wishlist** – Bold title with filter chips below.

- **Search Active** – Input with back/cancel controls.

- **Filter Mode** – Filter button + chips visible.

***

## **Layout & Spacing**

- **Height:** 56px app bar + 44px status bar.

- **Padding:** 16px horizontal, 8px between icons.

- **Spacing between title & actions:** 24px min.

- **Filter chips row:** 48px below app bar.

***

## **Behavior**

- **Back arrow:** Navigates to previous screen.

- **Trailing actions:** Toggle layouts or open menus.

- **Search input:** Replaces title on focus.

- **Cancel (Bağla):** Clears search and restores title.

- **Scroll behavior:** Optional elevation/shadow on scroll.

***

## **Usage Guidelines**

- Always show title or search field (never empty state).

- Limit trailing icons to 2 actions for clarity.

- Use bold title for primary screens (e.g., Wishlist).

- Display filter chips only if sorting options are available.

- Ensure tap targets meet 44px accessibility minimum.
